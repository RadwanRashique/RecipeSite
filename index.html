Gulam Jam

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  • Combine 2 cups of finely diced jab berries with 1 cup of julab syrup.
  • Gently fold in 1 tablespoon of lemon zest and 1/2 teaspoon of ginger powder.
  • Marinate the mixture for at least 30 minutes for enhanced flavors.
  • Transfer to a serving dish, garnish with mint leaves, and enjoy!
